Install Wallet File

Mention pre req having cold card and node synced
Cold Card Seeds Created 
First go to cold card
Then choose advanced > File Management > Export Wallet 
Here you can choose between a few standards
For our example in BTCPAY choose Electrum Wallet
Then Native-Segwit, you'll get a confirmation the wallet has been exported 
Now remove the micro sd and place it into the machine you accessing btcpay server
In this example we're ake a new store for this cold card wallet file
Choose Set Up wallet 
Import Wallet file
Cold Card Advanced ❯ MicroSD Card ❯ Export Wallet ❯ Electrum Wallet
Choose that exported wallet file
Check your addresses against your cold card 
On you cold card choose Address Explorer then confirm by hitting 4 and chose the address that starts with a "B"
This is the address type BTCPay will default generate. 
If you've done this correctly the addresses on screen and addresses on your cold card should match.
Click confirm to complete the wallet import. 
If you'd like to double check you've got it workiung check Xpub in your dashboard against the Xpub shown on your cold card screen.

Video 2
Create PSBT & Sign PBST

To spend bitcoin from these address you can do so right in side of BTCPAY
Under wallets go to bitcoin, then click Send 
Enter the destination adress, amount, fee rate. If you want to empty this adress click the "avilible balance"
If you already have partial signed bitcoin transaction click PSBT
Otherwise continue on to "sign transaction" to start the PSBT
For our use with Cold Card specificly we're going to select Partially Signed Bitcoin Transaction
At the top under trnasactions you'll see the amount your spending and some additional information under inputs
Until this transaction is signed you'll see a warning sybmol.
Move past sign transaction for now and click the down arrow to "export PSBT for signing"
Click download PSBT file, this will auto be named psbt-export.psbt.
Keep this page open in your browser while you doing the following steps.
Go to your downloads and I like to rename mine to make it easier to see which PSBT on my cold card
Save that to a micro SD Go back to cold card and select ready to sign. 
Find you PSBT on your coldcard and and follow the signing steps.
Then re insert your micro sd into the machine your accessing btcpay server.
Return to the transaction details page click the drop down for Provide updated PSBT.
Choose file and locate your micro SD with the PSBT and click open.
Next click Decode PSBT and you'll notice page change to confrim broadcasting this transaction.
Finally click broadcast your transaction and your bitcoin will be spent.
You'll be retured to your bitcoin wallet settings page with a transaction ID
Select this ID and go to mempool.space if you'd like to watch the transaction confirmations
other wise you can see your recent transactions back on the main dashboard.
